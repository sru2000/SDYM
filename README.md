# KrishiMitra AI – Agro Intelligence Platform

KrishiMitra AI is an AI-assisted agricultural intelligence and operational analytics platform designed to help agri-retail managers, field teams, and agricultural coordinators monitor crop conditions, identify risks, and generate intelligent recommendations through a centralized digital dashboard.

The platform simulates enterprise-scale agricultural intelligence workflows using dataset-driven analytics, backend processing, and AI-inspired recommendation systems focused on crop health, irrigation stress, pest outbreaks, operational tracking, and field management.

# Project Vision

Agriculture operations often suffer from fragmented data systems, delayed field insights, poor visibility into pest outbreaks, irrigation stress, and inefficient coordination between field teams and agri-retail stakeholders.

KrishiMitra AI aims to centralize these operations into a single intelligent dashboard capable of:
- Monitoring crop conditions
- Tracking regional agricultural risks
- Generating advisory recommendations
- Supporting field operations
- Simulating AI-driven agricultural decision systems

The platform is designed as a scalable foundation that can later integrate real-time APIs, IoT devices, satellite feeds, and advanced ML pipelines.
